Explore Capacity-Based Support

Service Delivery Aligned to System Size & Operational Risk

Partner-supported, white-label service delivery aligned to system size and operational risk, supporting partners in delivering services to their customers.

GWT Trading structures its service delivery around capability and capacity, ensuring the right level of technical ownership, response capability, and operational support is applied at every stage of a critical asset's lifecycle.

Rather than a one-size-fits-all approach, our model scales across three critical power ranges, allowing partners to deliver services confidently under their own brand — supported by ISO-aligned execution and transparent reporting via ProReports™.

Our Approach

How Capacity-Based Support Works

  • System size determines delivery responsibility
  • Operational risk determines scope
  • Partners retain customer ownership
  • GWT provides execution, response capability, and reporting

This model ensures clarity for partners, vendors, and customers alike — particularly in live, high-risk environments.

Our Philosophy

Uptime-Focused Support

In critical environments, the true measure of support capability is not how often maintenance is scheduled, but how effectively a provider can respond at the moment of failure.

GWT Trading's service model is weighted toward uptime protection, live-site response, and operational recovery, rather than periodic maintenance alone.

Our capabilities are structured around rapid mobilisation, technical execution under pressure, and controlled intervention within live environments — ensuring partners can meet their customers' continuity expectations when it matters most.

Why We Scale

Capacity-Aligned Delivery Model

This uptime-first philosophy is also why GWT self-delivers full lifecycle services at smaller system sizes, while operating in a vendor-aligned support role for larger, high-capacity systems.

In distributed and site-level environments, spare parts availability, system familiarity, and response logistics allow GWT to deliver rapid intervention and restoration directly. As system size and complexity increase, effective recovery becomes increasingly dependent on manufacturer-specific parts, specialised tooling, and OEM-controlled support pathways.

In these environments, MTTR is best achieved by supporting vendors and integrators who control those resources. GWT aligns its delivery model accordingly — ensuring the fastest, safest, and most reliable outcome for live, mission-critical systems.

Capacity Ranges We Support

Three Tiers of Service Delivery

0–10 kVA

Distributed & Site-Level Critical Power

Partner-Supported End-to-End Delivery, Maintenance & Support

GWT supports partners with full lifecycle services for distributed and site-level critical power systems, enabling them to deliver installation, support, and recovery capability to their customers with confidence.

Typical Environments

  • Communications rooms
  • Server rooms and edge IT
  • Commercial and operational facilities

Capability at This Range

  • Specialised freight and asset positioning
  • Electrical and mechanical installation
  • Site Acceptance Testing (SAT), start-up and functional testing
  • Optional monitoring and asset visibility
  • Flexible maintenance contract options (Basic / Comprehensive)
  • Service Level Agreements (SLAs) with on-site response and MTTR
  • Transparent reporting and documentation

Delivery Model: Partner-supported execution. All services delivered under partner branding with reporting via ProReports™.

11–40 kVA

Enterprise & Commercial Critical Power

Scalable Partner Delivery & Lifecycle Support

As systems increase in size and operational importance, GWT provides enterprise-grade delivery and structured lifecycle support, aligned to selected vendor-supported models.

Typical Environments

  • Enterprise IT environments
  • Healthcare and education facilities
  • Commercial critical infrastructure

Capability at This Range

  • Coordinated freight, handling, and site logistics
  • Electrical and mechanical installation
  • Commissioning, integration, and controlled changeovers
  • Lifecycle maintenance and operational support*
  • Monitoring, alarming, and performance visibility
  • Flexible maintenance contract options (Basic / Comprehensive*)
  • Compliance, reporting, and asset documentation
  • Battery testing and performance verification
  • Site Acceptance Testing (SAT)

Delivery Model: Partner-supported execution. All services delivered under partner branding with reporting via ProReports™.

* Commissioning and maintenance services available for selected vendor-supported models only, subject to manufacturer approval.

41–1000 kVA

High-Capacity & Mission-Critical Power

Vendor-Aligned Installation & Live-Site Support

For large-scale and mission-critical systems, GWT operates in a vendor-aligned support role, providing specialist capability inside live environments where OEM resources, spares, and tooling drive recovery outcomes.

Typical Environments

  • Data centres
  • Transport and aviation infrastructure
  • Large-scale commercial and industrial facilities

Capability at This Range

  • Specialised heavy freight and asset positioning
  • Large-scale battery handling, testing support, and replacement assistance
  • Vendor-aligned electrical and mechanical installation
  • Lift & shift and equipment relocation
  • Second-person and specialist contractor support
  • Installation assistance and site readiness works
  • Commissioning assistance (vendor-led)
  • Project reporting and delivery documentation

Delivery Model: Vendor-aligned, partner-supported execution. White-label project documentation via ProReports™.
