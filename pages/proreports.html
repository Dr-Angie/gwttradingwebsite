ProReports™

Professional, White-Label Reporting for Critical Infrastructure

The reporting platform underpinning all GWT Trading services — delivering transparent, audit-ready documentation under your brand.

Your Brand, Professional Delivery

ProReports™ is GWT Trading's white-label reporting platform, designed to deliver professional, consistent, and audit-ready documentation for all services performed on behalf of our partners.

Every report, asset record, and service document can be presented under your brand, ensuring your customers see a seamless, professional service experience — regardless of who performs the work.

Transparency. Consistency. Partner-aligned.

Platform Capabilities

What ProReports™ Delivers

Service Reports

Detailed reports for maintenance visits, installations, commissioning, and fault response activities.

Asset Registers

Comprehensive asset records including installation data, serial numbers, warranty status, and lifecycle history.

Monthly Overviews

Regular asset status reports covering monitored sites, alarm summaries, and performance trends.

Compliance Documentation

Audit-ready records for regulatory, warranty, and contractual compliance requirements.

Monitoring & Alarms

Visibility into monitored assets including alarm history, event logs, and performance data.

Project Documentation

Structured handover packs for installations, rollouts, and lifecycle replacement projects.

Partner-Aligned

White-Label by Design

ProReports™ is built specifically for white-label delivery. All outputs can be configured with:

  • Partner logo and branding
  • Partner contact details
  • Custom report headers and footers
  • Partner-specific terminology
  • Custom distribution lists
  • Branded customer portals (where applicable)

Your customers see your brand. GWT remains behind the scenes.
