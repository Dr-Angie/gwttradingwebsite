Our Services

Partner-Supported Critical Infrastructure Services

White-label service delivery aligned to system size and operational risk, supporting partners in delivering services to their customers.

GWT Trading provides behind-the-scenes critical infrastructure services for organisations operating in live, high-risk environments. All services are delivered in support of our partners, under their brand, using ISO-aligned systems and professional reporting via ProReports™.

Our services are designed to scale across critical power, precision cooling, logistics, and lifecycle support, with clear responsibility aligned to system size and operational risk.

Our Approach

How Our Services Are Structured

GWT services are organised in two complementary ways, allowing partners to engage flexibly while maintaining clarity around ownership, risk, and lifecycle responsibility.

What We Deliver

Service Capabilities

Critical Environment Logistics & Asset Movement

Specialised freight and asset movement services designed for live, operational environments where precision, planning, and risk control are critical.

  • Sensitive and high-value equipment transport
  • Live-site access, staging, and positioning
  • Heavy freight, rigging, and lift & shift operations
  • Free Issue equipment handling and logistics coordination
  • White-label delivery documentation via ProReports™

Electrical Installation

Licensed electrical installation services supporting critical power systems across a range of capacities.

  • UPS installation (single- and three-phase)
  • Bypass, protection, and distribution works
  • Integration with existing electrical infrastructure
  • Capacity-aligned delivery and vendor-supported works
  • White-label electrical documentation and handover packs

Critical Environment Technicians

Specialist technical support for work performed inside live, risk-managed environments.

  • Live-site technical execution
  • Site auditing and technical assessments
  • Second-person and specialist contractor support
  • OEM- and integrator-aligned delivery assistance
  • White-label audit and activity reporting

Mechanical Systems & Precision Cooling

Mechanical and cooling services supporting environments where temperature control is mission-critical.

  • Precision cooling installation and integration
  • CRAC, in-row, and supplementary cooling systems
  • Live-site mechanical works
  • Vendor-aligned mechanical installation support
  • White-label mechanical documentation

Battery Systems

Battery services supporting critical power reliability and lifecycle planning.

  • Battery installation, handling, and removal
  • Capacity and impedance testing
  • Battery monitoring systems installation and support
  • Vendor-directed replacement assistance
  • White-label battery reports and asset records

Commissioning, Testing & Planned Lifecycle Replacements

Controlled technical activities performed within live environments, aligned to system size and vendor requirements.

  • UPS start-up and functional testing
  • Site Acceptance Testing (SAT)
  • Planned lifecycle replacement activities
  • Vendor-led commissioning and replacement support
  • White-label commissioning and replacement documentation

Maintenance, Predictive Monitoring & Operational Support

Lifecycle support services for supported capacity ranges, focused on reliability and continuity.

  • Preventative maintenance programs
  • Predictive maintenance for monitored sites
  • Corrective maintenance and fault response
  • Basic and comprehensive maintenance contracts
  • SLA-backed response and MTTR commitments
  • White-label service and compliance reporting

Monitoring, Reporting & Asset Visibility (ProReports™)

The reporting platform underpinning all GWT services.

  • White-label, ISO-aligned reporting
  • Asset registers and lifecycle documentation
  • Monitoring, alarming, and performance visibility
  • Monthly asset overview reporting
  • Project-specific reporting for rollouts and audits

  • Documentation, reporting, and handover assistance
